First Step

Go to Google Takeout to download your Location History data: on that page, deselect everything except Location History by clicking "Select none" and then reselecting "Location History". Then, hit "Next" and, finally, click "Create archive". Once the archive has been created, click "Download".

Second Step

Unzip the downloaded file, and open the "Location History" folder within. Then, select "Upload" from the menu above.

Third Step

Select the areas you want to exclude (optional). Notice that "Patras Gazer", automatically, does not insert data other than the city of Patras (> 10km from coordinates 38.230462,21.753150). Then click "Next". Click "browse" to select your file or drag and drop it. If your .json file is up to 30 mb this might take a while. Please wait.
